What is a Security Token Offering (STO)?

Security token offerings are the digitisation of assets.


Popular assets in STOs: tokenisation of real estate and properties, fund shares, agricultural produce and data. However, tokenisation of assets is not limited to these. Any item can be tokenised as long as it produces returns to investors or token holders.

What qualifies as securities?


  • Promising profits or returns to investors without direct work done.

  • Tradable tokens with no utility in its ecosystem.

  • Examples are assets and securities such as bonds, property, fine art pieces and gold.

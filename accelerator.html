Novum Global Accelerator


Gain access to extensive resources, experienced mentors and best practices to launching your very own company.


During our 3-month accelerator programme, you will be coached to maximize operations and pushed to outperform.


We are looking for a coachable, responsible and innovative team which fall within our pre-requisites. In our first cohort, we will be accepting up to 15 teams.
